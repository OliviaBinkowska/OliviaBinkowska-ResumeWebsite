Hello and Welcome! I am currently a third year English major at the University of California, Riverside. As an undergraduate who has come in to the university system with outside credits, I have found that my degree completion date has been expedited. I am now in the process of weighing my options between graduating in Spring 2020, or declaring a second major in Education, Society, and Human Development. Regardless of my graduation date, my next steps are to begin the process of completing my Masters Degree in Education with a Teaching Credential in English.

My lifelong goal is to become a high school senior English teacher as well as a small business owner. I am in the process of completing my 200 hour Yoga Teacher Training certification through the Yoga Alliance with David Gramling, of Yoga & Crafts, LLC. In taking charge of my ambitious future, I am starting my journey of a teaching lifestyle and beginning to conduct my own range of Yoga classes with a basis of Chiropractic patients, and am assisting in classes not of my own in order to expand my knowledge and become the best Yogi that I could possibly be.

To support myself through college, I have landed a great job at Float State in Corona, CA. Float State is a modern float spa which offers float therapy/sensory deprivation tanks, infrared sauna, halotherapy/salt cave, cryotherapy, and Yoga!
